=======================
Pin Definition (QFN40)
=======================

BL604 40-pin package includes 10 power pins, 6 analog pins, 1 reset pin, and 23 flexible GPIO pins.


.. figure:: picture/pinlayout_40_L.png
   :align: center

   BL604L pin layout 

.. figure:: picture/pinlayout_40_CE.png
   :align: center

   BL604C/E pin layout
