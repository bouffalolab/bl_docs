========================
Pin Definition (QFN32)
========================

BL602 32-pin package includes 10 power pins, 6 analog pins, and 16 flexible GPIO pins.

.. figure:: ../../picture/Pinlayout32L.png
   :align: center

   BL602L pin layout

.. figure:: ../../picture/Pinlayout32CE.png
   :align: center

   BL602C/E pin layout
