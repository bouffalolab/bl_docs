
寄存器描述
====================

+----------------+-------------------------+
| 名称           | 描述                    |
+----------------+-------------------------+
| `l1c_config`_  | L1C configuration       |
+----------------+-------------------------+
| `hit_cnt_lsb`_ | Low 32-bit hit couter   |
+----------------+-------------------------+
| `hit_cnt_msb`_ | High 32-bit hit counter |
+----------------+-------------------------+
| `miss_cnt`_    | Miss counter            |
+----------------+-------------------------+
